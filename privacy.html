Curio Bot — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Curio Bot collects, uses, and stores information when users interact with it through Discord.

1. Information Curio May Collect

Depending on enabled features, Curio Bot may process or store:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server and channel IDs
  • Discord usernames or display names
  • Command names and interaction timestamps
  • Bot-generated records, settings, achievements, collections, points, or balances
  • Support requests, applications, reports, or information voluntarily submitted by users
  • Administrative and security logs
  • Torn player names or public player IDs when voluntarily provided or required by a feature

Curio Bot will never request Discord passwords, Torn passwords, authentication codes, or other private account credentials.

2. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Operate commands and community features
  • Maintain persistent user records
  • Prevent spam, abuse, duplicate processing, or fraud
  • Provide achievements, rankings, collections, and rewards
  • Investigate bugs and security incidents
  • Process support requests
  • Improve Curio Bot's reliability and community experience

3. Data Sharing

Curio Bot does not sell personal information.

Information may be viewed by authorized bot operators or trusted community staff when necessary to operate, secure, troubleshoot, moderate, or improve the service.

4. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to operate Curio Bot, preserve community records, resolve disputes, improve security, or maintain historical features.

5. Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect stored information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. User Requests

Users may request correction or deletion of their stored Curio Bot data where reasonably possible.

Some information may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, auditing, or dispute resolution.

7. Children

Curio Bot is intended only for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements.

8. Third-Party Services

Curio Bot operates through Discord and may interact with third-party services. Those services maintain their own Terms and Privacy Policies.

9. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may change as Curio Bot evolves. The newest version will always be published here.

10. Contact

Questions or requests regarding privacy may be submitted through Curio Bot's official Discord support server.
